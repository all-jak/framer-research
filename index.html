E-commerce Templates: The Top Opportunity

"The e-commerce niche stands out as the most significant opportunity in the Framer template market as of mid-2025."

With a substantial monthly search volume of approximately 2,900 and a relatively small pool of only 138 live templates, this category boasts an impressive opportunity score of 61.7 [357].

The gap between the number of people searching for e-commerce solutions and the number of quality templates available suggests that the market is far from saturated. This presents a unique chance for a well-designed, feature-rich e-commerce template to quickly gain traction.

Success Factors

  • Payment gateway integration
  • Inventory management systems
  • Mobile-first design approach
  • Conversion-focused layouts

Landing Page Templates: High Demand and Potential

Landing page templates represent another highly profitable niche within the Framer ecosystem. These templates are in constant demand from startups, SaaS companies, and marketing teams who need to launch campaigns and validate product ideas quickly.

The key to success in this category is to create templates that are not only visually stunning but also optimized for conversions. This includes strategically placed call-to-action buttons, compelling hero sections, and sections for testimonials and feature highlights.

Strategic CTAs

Conversion-optimized button placement and design

Hero Sections

Compelling above-the-fold content that engages visitors

Social Proof

Testimonials and feature highlights that build trust

European Market Insights

Market Characteristics

The European market for Framer templates is a significant and growing segment, with Framer's origins in the Netherlands giving it a strong foothold in the region [333].

Cultural Design Preferences
